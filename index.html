About me

Below I've provided small points to inform of my strong skill sets with detail and information about how and why I am skilled at these things.

Programming

During my studies at university I attended lectures on computer science which further drew me into game design. After graduating I proceeded to delve deeper into software development with an apprenticeship focusing on studying c# alongside working on a cross-platform mobile project.

2d Art

Whilst taking a break from university I focused on hobbies and what I enjoyed, again leading myself to creative opportunities. I began to find a love of art and drawing attending life drawing classes and engaging myself in both vector, painting, and concept drawing.

3d Art

For a year in my college education I studied Computer games development. It largely focused on the fundamentals of 3d art. From this developed a massive hobby that is a passion of mine. From the study I have gained skills in modelling, soft surface and hard. As well as texturing, rigging and animating.

Music and Sound

After my college education I took a step away from the 3d aspects and began focusing on music and sound. The reason was that I felt that the course that I was on wouldn't be the best route into game design and I felt an academic education would better my chances at breaking into the industry than a vocational one.

Game Design

Finally with all this combined has developed my skills into game design. Not only do I enjoy the creative processes but I have an unending passion for games, I engage in both board games and video. With a guilty pleasure of D and D enjoying the imagination and comradary that it provides

Writing

Whilst at university I engaged within the drama society of my university performing in plays and engaging in many elements of performance. Whilst doing this an opportunity arose in which I wrote a comedic play that won best of that year.
